Our Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

This stage introduces foundational concepts like numbers, letters, and shapes, encouraging cognitive and emotional development. Through interactive activities and structured routines, children build essential skills for a smooth transition to primary school.

Elementary School

Elementary School provides a strong academic foundation, focusing on core subjects like reading, writing, mathematics, and science. It emphasizes the development of critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork skills through engaging and age-appropriate activities.

Middle (10-16) Years

High School prepares students for higher education and future careers by offering advanced coursework and skill-building opportunities. It emphasizes personal growth, critical thinking, and responsibility, helping students explore their interests and set future goals.
